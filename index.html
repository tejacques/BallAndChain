The Proposal

After six wonderful years together, on a crisp evening December 20, 2015, after dining at Eleven Madison Park, Natasha and Thomas were asked if they would like to have a private tour of the kitchen for the final dessert of apple caramel drizzled over shaved ice.


Next they were escorted to a table at the back, and by the dim glow of candelight to the tune of "When I fall in Love" by Nat King Cole Natasha's Photo album highlighting their years together was brought to the table. As Natasha began to realize what was going on, Thomas got down on one knee and asked for her hand in marriage.
